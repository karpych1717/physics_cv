Research

When I was at bachelor and master I used to simulate bacteria density movement within the Keller-Segal-like model.

Bachelor`s degree work illustrates a density response in a one-dimensional system while repellent concentration is a linear coordinate function.

In master`s degree work, it is assumed that a single chemical may be both attractant or repellent, and bacteria can decrease its concentration by eating. The formation of the aerotactic band and its semi-periodic movement were shown.

Now investigating the properties of bosons within the Skyrme-like mean-field model. The most recent publication:
