Research

When I was at bachelor and master I used to simulate bacteria density movement within the Keller-Segal-like model.

Bachelor`s degree work illustrates a response of the density in a one-dimentional system while repellent concentration is a linear coordinate function.

In master`s degree work, it is assumed that a single chemical may be both attractant or repellent, and bacteria can decrease its concentration by eating. The formation of the aerotactic band and its semiperiodic movement were shown.

Now investigating the properties of bosons within the Skyrme-like mean-field model. The most recent publication:
